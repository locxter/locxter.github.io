On the beauty of biking

2023-01-07

Besides my deep love for all kinds of technology and Sci-Fi books/movies, I really enjoy biking on every occasion possible. In this article I will try to present you the beauty I see in biking...

First and foremost, biking is an excellent way to get from one location to another . It is super cheap as you do not have to pay for any fuel, healthy since you are at the fresh air as well as quite fast (especially in the city where cars often have to stop at traffic lights). Even rainy weather can not stop you with proper clothing or alternatively enough mental strength.

Besides that, cycling also is an amazing sport for people of all ages, because almost everyone already owns a bike and therefore does not have to invest large amounts of money to get started, it can be a very social activity when biking with others and it is very gentle on the joints.

Last but certainly not least, riding a bike also allows you to explore your surrounding area due its relatively large operation radius compared to walking or running. I am in the very fortunate situation of living in mid-sized city in the beautiful Ammerland where I only have to drive 2 km to get to the middle of nowhere and biking allows me to explore the incredible beauty of local nature.

If you want to become more active this year, cycling is very good and low-effort possibility to do so. Just get started...
